About us

Our story

Formed in 2009, Bill’s Bookkeeping Services, LLC, strives to fill an increasing need in the marketplace. As the economy has weakened, and profit margins have tightened, BBS provides an avenue for companies to outsource many of the activities associated with everyday business administration. This allows our clients to reduce direct overhead expenses while still receiving quality performance of day-to-day tasks such as marketing, financial recording and reporting, processing and payment of invoices, basic payroll processing and benefits administration, and so much more! We can modify and mold our services to best fit your business needs – just call us today so we can begin the process of getting to know one another.
